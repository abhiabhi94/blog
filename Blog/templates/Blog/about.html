About

HackAdda is an online blog that aims to increase the technical awareness around the world. We bring you the latest developments in the field of Science, Programming, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, {% comment %} Augmented Reality, {% endcomment %} {% comment %} Games, {% endcomment %} and many more. Most articles will fall under the real of STEAM(Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics).

We aim to become your one-stop destination for most technology-related curiosities.

The featured section contains the editors pick for our readers. They can explore trending, latest articles.

There are different categories to explore. There are cheatsheets on various tools, tutorial series for various concepts, advice for following best practices while developing different open source softwares, web applications, tools, cross-platform software, etc. Each article has a category along with a couple of tags.

We also have a section dedicated for kids. Since they are bound to become the leaders of tommorrow, we aim to develop their interest in the field of STEAM. The kid section looks to explore with the practices that make learning STEAM an enjoyable experience.

Readers may also Log in and bookmark their favorite articles.

Currently, we have not opened the forum for accepting articles from people outside our team. We would love to broaden our team of hackers. We are working on that front, till then, in case you are interested in being part of our team, you may apply along with your application to emailaddress@hackadda.com.

We look forwarding to hearing from you.
